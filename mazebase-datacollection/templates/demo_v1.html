Instructions

Welcome to the HIT! Our goal is to collect annotations of you solving a step-by-step problem in a crafting-based maze game. Given a goal, you want to break the task down into steps, write each down and execute in sequence. Think of how you would explain a step of a larger task to a 5 year old, demonstrate how to do it, then continue until you have accomplished the overall task. This paired text and execution sequence is what we are looking for!

When the board appears, press the start game button to load task and you will see a screen similar to the one below. I will walk you through each portion of the page in subsequent steps. If you do not follow the instructions below, we reserve the right to not pay for the HIT. If you have ALREADY completed the HIT, feel free to skip the demo by scrolling to the bottom and hitting continue.

Evaluate your assigned goal and helpful recipes. Take a look at what is currently on the board and how you can use what is there to help you complete the goal!

If you do not comply with these instructions, you will not get paid: After evaluating your goal, decide what your first instruction is and write that in the instruction box. (1) Keep it high level and break the task down, but do not write out specific button presses. (2) Do not only write just 1 instruction for the entire task. Then hit enter.

Use UP, LEFT, DOWN, RIGHT (or arrow keys) to navigate character. Use the other buttons, explained below to complete the task.


Don't worry, if you forget, press "Helpful Hints" on the gamepage for this refresher again

After you have completed the first instruction, press the "DONE" button (or hit enter). Then continue to write the next instruction, submit, and then repeat until goal is completed.
